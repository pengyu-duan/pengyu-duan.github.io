I'm a fourth-year PhD student from Institute of Fluid Dynamics, [Beihang University](https://ev.buaa.edu.cn/), studying the physics, scaling, and control of wall-bounded turbulence, with particular emphasis on dissipation mechanisms, circulation statistics, and multiscale energy transfer. My research combines direct numerical simulations and theoretical analysis to understand how turbulent structures interact across scales and with boundaries.
I work with Prof. Xi Chen.
